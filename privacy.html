Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

CreatorPost ("we", "our", or "us") is a desktop content management tool that helps creators publish videos to TikTok. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account via OAuth, we receive your TikTok username, display name, avatar, and open ID as authorized by you through TikTok's Login Kit.
  • Access Tokens: We store OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens locally on your device to maintain your session. These tokens are never transmitted to any third-party server.
  • Video Content: Videos you choose to upload are transmitted directly from your device to TikTok's servers via TikTok's Content Posting API. We do not store copies of your videos on any server.
  • Post Metadata: Titles, descriptions, hashtags, and privacy settings you configure for your posts are stored locally on your device and transmitted to TikTok during the publishing process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your TikTok account and maintain your login session.
  • To display your account information within the application interface.
  • To publish video content to TikTok on your behalf, with your explicit consent for each post.
  • To check the publishing status of your content.

3. Data Storage and Security

All user data, including access tokens and post history, is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any backend servers that store your personal data. Your TikTok credentials are encrypted at rest on your local machine.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only transmitted to TikTok's official API endpoints as necessary to provide the core functionality of publishing content.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party service:

  • TikTok for Developers API: We use TikTok's Login Kit for authentication and Content Posting API for video publishing. Your use of these features is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Disconnect: You can revoke our application's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings or within our application.
  • Delete: Since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the application removes all stored data from your device.
  • Control: You have full control over every post. No content is published without your explicit action and consent.

7. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
